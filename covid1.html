Our Aim in Mission 1000

In today's world we want to bring everyone together and do our bit in breaking the Chain of COVID. With this vision we launch our Mission 1000. Are you a Home Isolated patient with RTPCR report not older than 5 days? If yes, then please register on BimaXpress website to be eligible for a free CORONA Kit being delivered to you to your doorstep.
